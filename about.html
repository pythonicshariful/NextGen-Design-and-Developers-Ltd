A legacy of excellence

A young man’s dream of making homeownership a joyful experience gave rise to the real estate developer known as building technology and ideas (bti) ltd. From its inception over 4 decades ago, bti has been a stalwart in Bangladeshi real estate, known for its high quality of construction, design excellence, reliability & customer-centricity. Our dedication and expertise with a focus on the pursuit of excellence have made us reliable to our Joint Venture Customers and our Homeowners. We believe not just in building relationships, but also nurturing and caring for them, year after year. We are truly humbled by our customers’ trust, which has only grown steadily since 1984.

Let us make your dream home come to life, ensuring on-time handover and the best value for money. You can also get in touch if you are looking to sell your land in a prime location in Dhaka or Chattogram.
